Radiation Dosage Guide

Accumulated Dose

10,000mSv Fatal dose.
5,000mSv   50% fatal in 1 month.
1,000mSv   Radiation sickness symptoms. 

Safe Limits

100mSv per year   (11uS/Hr)        Increased cancer risk
50mSv per year     (5.7uSv/Hr)     Maximum "safe" level per year
20mSv per year    (2.3uSv/Hr)     Maximum allowed for nuclear employees (HSE IRR99)
2.7mSv per year   (0.31uSv/Hr)    UK average total dose per year
2.3mSv per year   (0.26uSv/Hr)    UK average dose due to radiation in the environment per year
1.1mSv per year   (0.12uSv/Hr)    Background level in Sussex, UK
